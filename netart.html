NET ART

For this project I was inspired to do something poetic, and I combined my love for spoken word and education in one place. I often feel with technology being so present in our world, we forget how to interact with one another and make each other feel loved and known. I was inspired by the words of my favorite spoken word artist, Phil Kaye, who preformed "Beginning, Middle, and End." It is a narrative about how we are all different, but there is beauty in it. So in my net art, I poetically remind the viewers how to value one another as it always should be.
