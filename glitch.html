GLITCH ART

If there is anything that is a Glitch it would be the year 2020. I wanted to represent how 2020 has made me feel. A distortion of WHAT I SHOULD BE DOING and WHERE I SHOULD BE and DREAMS I SHOULD BE SOWING. Like everyone else, life feels glitched and some how we put in the wrong code for 2020.

The first image is a self portrait of myself making ceramics, but it is not really obvious of what you're looking at. It is funny that only I know what I am looking at because it is my distorted world.

Second image is my travel plans for summer 2020 being disintegrated into pieces.

Third image I chose a business sign glitched it with replacing html code, audacity, and changed the color of the photo through Adobe Lightroom. It is my dream to opening an etsy shop or online shop of some kind to sell my ceramics one day.
